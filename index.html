Deterministic Safety

Non-negotiable physical envelope

Stabilizer consumes real-time telemetry and enforces immediate protective actions when thresholds are crossed. This path is isolated and does not wait for long-horizon forecasts.

  • Physics-first constraints are enforced locally and instantly.
  • Hard stop / safe setpoints are deterministic.
  • Designed to remain robust under partial system failures.
Public-safe outputs

What is shown publicly

Only conceptual control behavior is described here. Sensitive parameters, thresholds, and internal telemetry schemas remain undisclosed.

  • No equipment-specific tuning disclosed.
  • No operational thresholds published.
  • No proprietary integration details exposed.
Forecasting Plane

Probabilistic insights, not control

Forecasting produces hypotheses about yield and drift trends. Its outputs are always gated and bounded before any operational consequence.

  • Runs near-line / off-line.
  • Outputs are confidence-scored and reviewed by governance gates.
  • Never writes setpoints directly.
Interpretability

Operationally readable signals

The system aims to output operational signals that can be audited and compared to historical outcomes, avoiding opaque “trust me” recommendations.

  • Confidence + consistency scoring.
  • Change justification is logged.
  • Bounded-by-policy action mapping.
Governed Orchestration

Policy gates and routing

Supervisory control merges deterministic safety with probabilistic insights under strict policy constraints, selecting allowed actions and rejecting unsafe paths.

  • Applies sovereignty constraints and locality boundaries.
  • Ensures any recommendation remains reversible when required.
  • Coordinates escalation modes for stability.
Audit

Traceability by design

Operational decisions are recorded for governance review. Publicly, we describe the concept only—internal ledger format and keys remain confidential.

  • Decision provenance.
  • Change history and rollback checkpoints.
  • Compliance-oriented reporting (public-safe).
Bounded Actions

Gradient + checkpoints + rollback

Economic Guard shapes any optimization proposal into gradual steps with checkpoints, preventing large swings triggered by uncertain forecasts.

  • No hard stops from probabilistic outputs.
  • Action ramps with rollback points.
  • Impact caps enforced by policy.
Public-safe assurance

What we disclose

We disclose the safety logic (bounded impact) and separation principles, not the thresholds, weights, or internal scoring formulas.

  • Separation: safety vs optimization.
  • Bounded-by-policy governance gates.
  • Audit presence without exposing internals.
